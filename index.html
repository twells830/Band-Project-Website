Drax Project is a four piece Alternative Pop/Hip-Hop band who began as group of buskers on the streets of Wellington in 2012. Selling out their EP release show in Wellington, along with being announced on the line up for Rhythm & Alps and Rhythm & Vines NZ this year, Drax Project are a name to keep an eye out for.

Shaan, Vocal-Sax-Synth / Sam, Bass-Vocal / Ben, Guitar-Vocal / Matt, Drums-Cymbals
